About DoBu Martial Arts

Welcome to DoBu Martial Arts, your premier destination for martial arts, fitness, and self-defense training. Our state-of-the-art facility boasts a large, matted martial arts area, a fully-equipped gym, a relaxing sauna, a rejuvenating steam room, and modern changing and shower facilities.

We are dedicated to fostering discipline, strength, and community. Our experienced instructors are passionate about guiding students of all ages and skill levels on their journey to physical and mental mastery. Join us and discover the transformative power of martial arts.

Judo

A modern martial art, combat, and Olympic sport created in Japan. Its most prominent feature is its competitive element, where the object is to either throw or takedown an opponent to the ground.
